    For the past five years Johnny has been an active member at his local community centre. He is a member of the community centre's event planning committee. They plan events that are either free, or inexpensive for young families (children 3-5 years of age).     About

    Johnny is a community leader, active volunteer, and aspiring entrepreneur. He has worked with many non-profit organizations aimed towards bettering society, allowing him to work with a huge demographic. He has worked with issues regarding poverty and hunger to issues around modern-day education. He is the Co-Founder and Production Manager at Glass Half Full, a company focused on raising awareness on present-day water issues. When Johnny isn't working or volunteering for his community, he is a competitive dragon boater and has competed in the Canadian International Dragon Boat Festival for four consecutive years. As a pianist of nine years Johnny enjoys spending his free time immersed in music.
